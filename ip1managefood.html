Food Management Dashboard - Manager's View

Inventory Control

Managers need to keep track of available food items to avoid overstocking or shortages. Maintaining a proper balance in the inventory is key.

Quality Management

Ensure the food quality meets standards. Expired or low-quality items should be discarded. Regular checks for freshness are important.

Pricing Strategy

Managers determine pricing based on cost and competition. Ensure pricing reflects both quality and affordability.

Sales Performance

Monitor daily and weekly sales to assess which items are popular and how well they are performing in the market.

Manager's Additional Thoughts

Food management is not only about stock and pricing but also involves understanding customer preferences, seasonal trends, and cost efficiency. Managers should balance quality with cost and constantly monitor sales trends to make informed decisions.
